Sections are numbered automatically. Add or delete one and everything
after it renumbers itself.

\subsection{Emphasis}

Text can be \textbf{bold}, \textit{italic}, or \texttt{monospaced}
(handy for code). Nest them to combine: \textbf{\textit{both at once}}.

\subsection{Characters that need escaping}

A few characters mean something to LaTeX and must be escaped with a
backslash to appear literally: \% \$ \& \_ \#. A backslash itself is
\textbackslash{} (not \verb|\\|, which means "new line").

\section{Spaces and quotes}

Multiple     spaces in the source collapse to one. Quotation marks are
made from backticks and apostrophes, not the \texttt{"} key:
``like this'' and `like this'.
